the 3d rock archive:

a few years ago i began collecting 3d scans of rock climbing holds from the bouldering areas i visited.
the collection now includes scans from fontainebleau, rocklands, squamish, bishop, hueco tanks, and the laurentides of quebec.
at the same time, i developped an interest in blockchain technology.
thinking of what i should do with my expansive collection of pretty 3d objects,
i decided i wanted to juxtapose the raw natural-ness of the stone scans (that have already been 'perverted' by their digitization)
with the amorphous blockchain function.
i have began minting the scans on the tezos blockchain. the point is not to profit from the stone, i have minted one edition of each scan,
priced as the V-grade of the problem it came from. no scan would cost a collector more than $20USD to own.
link to my objkt profile to view the collection of scans i have minted so far.
you must create a tezos wallet in order to purchase and become part of the phenomenon of blockchain.
