the 3d rock archive:

a few years ago i began collecting 3d scans of rock climbing holds from the bouldering areas i visited. the collection now includes scans from around the world. i decided i wanted to juxtapose the raw natural-ness of the stone scans (that have already been altered by their digitization) with the amorphous phenomenon of blockchain technology. i have began minting the scans on the tezos blockchain to create a library of rock holds that is immortalized online.

link to my objkt profile to view the collection of scans i have minted so far.
