Saved Climbs

Legend

Exposure

  • Sometimes a feeling, sometimes an objective reality
  • Almost anything with lots of air beneath it will feel exposed
  • Certain positions on a wall like on a prow or very insecure slab have an exposed feeling
  • Ultimately, each climber decides what feels exposed to them

Risk

  • A complex and often subjective calculation of how likely a fall would be
  • Can depend on the skill of the climber, can be an intrinsic quality of a movement

Consequence

  • The result of a fall
  • Ranges from no consequence, to injury, to death at its most extreme
